# SoapSwirl Studio Privacy Policy and User Agreement

Locale: en-US  
Bundle ID: `com.soapswirl.studio`

## Privacy Policy

SoapSwirl Studio is local-only. It stores saved soap swirl sketch titles, scent notes, color choices, swirl intensity, pour style, cut-face notes, favorites, and generated local visual references in the app container on the user's device as JSON.

SoapSwirl Studio does not use tracking, analytics, advertising, backend accounts, cloud sync, camera, photo library, microphone, contacts, location, or external uploads. `PrivacyInfo.xcprivacy` declares no tracking and no collected data.

Starter examples and Premium Local Pack names are bundled/local metadata. They are not online recipes and do not connect to a live service.

## User Agreement

SoapSwirl Studio is a creative planning tool for handmade soap visuals. Users are responsible for checking real ingredients, safety, curing, labeling, and selling requirements outside the app before making or selling soap.

Premium Local Packs are an optional non-consumable unlock for extra local visual themes. Base creation remains free: Scent Color Setup, Swirl Path Sketcher, saving, editing, deleting, and the manual App Intent fallback are not blocked by Premium.

The app provides local creative notes and previews only. It does not provide professional, medical, legal, or manufacturing advice.
